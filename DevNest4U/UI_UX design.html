UI/UX Design

What is UI/UX?

UI (User Interface) : focuses on how a product looks.
UX (User Experience) : is about how it works and feels.

Where, When, and Why?

  • Where: Websites, Mobile Apps, Digital Products
  • When: During planning, designing, or redesigning phases
  • Why: To improve usability, engagement, and product value

Benefits of UI/UX

  • Improves user satisfaction
  • Reduces development cost
  • Boosts engagement and conversions
  • Builds trust and brand loyalty

Popular Tools

Figma

Collaborative browser-based UI design tool with prototyping and teamwork support.
Adobe XD

Design and prototype with rich features and Adobe ecosystem integration.
Sketch

Mac-only vector-based design tool perfect for clean interfaces and components.
